Step 1

Go to musescore.org and download MuseScore Studio.

Step 2

Launch MuseScore Studio and click "New score."

Step 3

Select the instruments you want to use. The "Family" dropdown box allows you to select instruments that are typically used in particular groups, such as marching band instruments. In addition, MuseScore offers pre-made template groups, which can be found under "Create from template."

Step 4

Here, there are palettes available that allow you to add various musical notations. If you cannot find what you are looking for, then you may want to look under "Add palettes," use the search bar, or create a custom palette under "Add palettes."
